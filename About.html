About

 

Greetings! I'm Isaiah Sisneros, a photographer from Albuquerque, New Mexico. With a five-year photographic journey,

I mostly capture my shots through the nostalgic allure of 35mm film, wielded skillfully by my trusty Minolta SRT-202. Occasionally, I venture into 120mm to add diversity to my visual storytelling.

My foray into photography began during my freshman year of high school when a serendipitous elective choice led me to a photography class.

What started as a random assignment soon blossomed into a lifelong passion. Throughout my high school years and beyond,

I relentlessly pursued the art of photography, earning accolades and recognition. Notably,

my work found a prestigious place in the Albuquerque Museum, and the culmination of my artistic journey led to an exhibition at the illustrious Carnegie Hall.

This unexpected and remarkable trajectory has not only shaped my identity as a photographer but has also allowed me to share my unique perspective with a broader audience.
