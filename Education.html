Education

I'm honored to share some highlights of my artistic journey. These accolades serve as milestones, reflecting personal achievements and the shared appreciation for the art we create together.

Several of my pieces have found their way into the prestigious Albuquerque Museum, an institution dedicated to celebrating and preserving the rich cultural tapestry of our region. It's a privilege to contribute to this collective narrative through my lens.

The Albuquerque Metro Show has been a rewarding arena where my work has been adorned with numerous medals. This local recognition is exceptional as it resonates with the vibrant artistic community that has been a constant source of inspiration.

On a national scale, I'm proud to have received a distinguished award from Scholastic. This recognition speaks to my work's universal appeal and impact, transcending geographical boundaries.

The media has also played a role in amplifying my artistic voice, with a notable news segment dedicated to my

photography. It's a humbling experience to see my creative endeavors shared with a broader audience,

and it motivates me to continue exploring new dimensionswithin my craft.


These awards and acknowledgments are not just milestones; they are a testament to

the support and encouragement from the community and beyond.

I am deeply grateful for the opportunities to share my perspective and artistry, and I look forward to continuing this journey of creative exploration.
